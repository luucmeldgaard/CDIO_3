Use Case: Win game

Scope: Junior Monopoly game
Level: User Goal
Primary Actor: Players
Stakeholders and interests: Players
Preconditions: Any player must make a move that results in them getting a negative balance
Success Guarantee: The game ends. The player with the highest balance wins the game.

Success scenario:
    1. A player has to pay an amount in which the player doesn't have funds for.
    2. No more turns can be made.
    3. All players' balance is sorted from lowest to highest.
    4. The player with the highest balance wins the game.
